Brand & Style
Pathshala AI embodies **Human-Centric Intelligence**—an AI that feels calm, trustworthy, and deeply personal rather than cold and robotic.
The visual language draws inspiration from golden-hour studying in nature, blending premium productivity software with editorial storytelling. Every screen should evoke the feeling of sitting in an open meadow at sunset with unlimited knowledge available, even without an internet connection.
The emotional pillars are:
• Calm Focus
• Wonder
• Optimistic Growth
• Curiosity
• Freedom to Learn Anywhere
The experience should feel less like an LMS and more like an intelligent companion.
The interface should always breathe with generous whitespace, floating surfaces, subtle glassmorphism, and soft ambient lighting.
Avoid anything that feels corporate, sterile, or overloaded.
---
Colors
The palette is inspired directly by the hero artwork.

Typography
Outfit provides a warm geometric personality suitable for a modern AI tutor.
Inter ensures exceptional readability during long learning sessions.
Large headings should remain bold with tight tracking to maintain an Apple-like premium aesthetic.
Body text should always maintain generous spacing for comfortable reading.
Never use more than two font families.
